Our Story

Rooted in tradition,
crafted with heart

Pu Dong Kitchen serves authentic Shanghainese cuisine, featuring handcrafted dumplings, comforting noodles, and rich braised specialties. Our menu blends traditional home-style flavours with refined classics, offering a balance of hearty dishes and lighter favourites.

Perfect for both casual dining and group gatherings, we deliver a true taste of Shanghai in a warm, welcoming setting right here in Singapore.
