Our Story

Born from fire,
built on passion

Ember & Oak was founded on a simple belief — that great food starts with honest ingredients and fearless technique. Our kitchen is anchored by a wood-fire hearth that imparts a depth of flavour no other cooking method can replicate.

Every dish on our menu is a dialogue between tradition and invention, crafted by our team of chefs who trained across three continents to bring you something truly unforgettable.
